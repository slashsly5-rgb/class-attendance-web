# Class Attendance: Student Guide

Welcome to the Class Attendance portal! This guide will explain how to enroll in your courses and check in for daily attendance using your smartphone or laptop.

## 1. Enrolling in a Class
To join a class for the semester, you only need to do this **once**.

1. Your lecturer will provide you with a **Class Code** (e.g., `CS101` or `MATH202`).
2. Navigate your browser to: `https://your-domain.com/enroll`.
3. Enter the Class Code exactly as provided.
4. Enter your **Full Name**.
5. Enter your **Student ID**.
6. Click **Enroll**.
7. If successful, you will see a green success screen and a button to proceed to your **Student Dashboard**.

## 2. The Student Dashboard
Your dashboard (`/student/dashboard`) is your homepage. Bookmarking this page is highly recommended. 

Here, you will see:
- A list of all classes you are currently enrolled in.
- Your personal attendance statistics (e.g., 5 Attended, 1 Late, 0 Absent).
- A large **Check In** button for active classes.

## 3. How to Check In
When your lecturer starts the class, they will project a random **6-Digit Passcode** on the screen.

1. Ensure you are physically inside the lecture hall.
2. Open your **Student Dashboard**.
3. Under the active class, click **Check In**.
4. The system will prompt your browser for **Location Permissions**. 
   - **CRITICAL**: You *must* click "Allow" on the browser popup. The system uses GPS (Haversine Distance) to prove you are actually in the room. If you block location access, you cannot submit attendance.
5. Enter the **6-Digit Passcode** shown on the projector.
6. Click **Submit**.

### Troubleshooting Location Issues
- **"Location access denied"**: You may have previously blocked location access for this site. Tap the small 'lock' or 'location' icon in your browser's URL bar and change the permission to "Allow". Reload the page.
- **"You are too far away"**: The GPS thinks you are outside the geofence radius. Ensure you aren't using a VPN that masks your location, and try stepping closer to the center of the room. Refresh your browser to get a newer, more accurate GPS lock.

## 4. Viewing Your History
At any time, you can click on a Class Card in your Dashboard to view your full history. It will show exactly which days you were marked Present, Late, or Absent.
